 Love and Hope Energy Balls
Step 1: Gather the ingredients needed. You will need ½ cup of oats, ½ cup of almond butter, 2 tablespoons of honey, 2 tablespoons of love, 1 teaspoon of hope, and 1 teaspoon of tiredness. 
Step 2: Place all ingredients in a medium bowl and mix together until combined.
Step 3: Refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes or until the mixture hardens enough to shape.
Step 4: Once the mixture is chilled, use a spoon or your hands to make small balls. 
Step 5: Place the balls onto a baking sheet and chill for an additional 10 minutes. 
Step 6: Enjoy your Love and Hope Energy Balls!
