 Love and Hope Energy Balls
Step 1: Gather the ingredients needed. You will need ½ cup of oats, ½ cup of almond butter, 2 tablespoons of honey, 2 tablespoons of love, 1 teaspoon of hope, and 1 teaspoon of tiredness. 
Step 2: Place all ingredients in a medium bowl and mix together until combined.
Step 3: Refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes or until the mixture hardens enough to shape.
Step 4: Once the mixture is chilled, use a spoon or your hands to make small balls. 
Step 5: Place the balls onto a baking sheet and chill for an additional 10 minutes. 
Step 6: Enjoy your Love and Hope Energy Balls!

 Steak with Porcini Mushrooms 
Step 1: Begin by preparing the steak. Pat the steak dry using a paper towel. Season the steak generously with salt and pepper.
Step 2: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once the butter and olive oil mixture has heated up, add the steak and cook it for 3-4 minutes per side, or until it has browned.
Step 3: Once the steak is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set it aside.
Step 4: Add an additional tablespoon of butter to the skillet, along with the garlic and porcini mushrooms. Cook for 3-4 minutes or until the mushrooms have softened. 
Step 5: Return the steak back to the skillet, and season the mixture with fresh thyme. Stir to combine everything together. 
Step 6: Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes or until the steak is cooked to your desired preference. 
Step 7: Serve the steak with the porcini mushrooms, and enjoy!

 Spicy Coconut Lime Chicken
Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). 
Step 2: In a shallow mixing bowl, combine the coconut milk, lime juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt and pepper. 
Step 3: Place the chicken breasts in the bowl, and gently turn several times to coat with the marinade. Cover the bowl and place in the refrigerator to marinate for 20 minutes.
Step 4: Transfer the chicken to a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish. 
Step 5: Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until the chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
Step 6: Remove from the oven, let stand 5 minutes, then serve. Enjoy!
